Research & Key Projects

GPS-Denied Autonomous UAV Navigation

Problem: keep a UAV localized and autonomous when GNSS is lost, jammed, or spoofed. I designed and implemented a family of complementary navigation methods, each solving a different operational scenario.

Absolute Visual Localization (map-matching)
  • Recover absolute position by matching the live downward camera stream to a georeferenced reference map (satellite tiles / orthomosaics) via feature matching and homography estimation — robust to scale, rotation, and seasonal appearance change.
Road-Based Navigation
  • Real-time road detection & tracking that keeps the road centered in the frame and closes the loop onto the autopilot, so the UAV follows road networks autonomously with no GPS fix.
Visual Teach & Repeat
  • Teach pass builds a geo-tagged feature map along the route; on the repeat pass the UAV re-localizes against it and automatically retraces the path the instant GPS drops — a practical fail-safe for return-to-home.
LTE Signal-Based Positioning
  • Trilateration from cellular tower signatures (ID, RSSI, type) against a known tower database — a vision-independent fallback for low-visibility conditions.
INS Dead-Reckoning + Vision Drift Correction
  • EKF fuses airspeed, IMU, barometer, and compass for short-term position estimation; unbounded INS drift is periodically reset using visual map-matching from downlinked imagery — combining the strengths of both estimators.
Real-Time Aerial Image Processing

Problem: raw UAV video is distorted, shaky, and unmapped — the geometric backbone every downstream navigation and mapping task depends on.

Camera Calibration & Undistortion
  • Intrinsic calibration (chessboard, circles-grid, ChArUco); estimation of camera matrix and distortion coefficients, with real-time undistortion feeding the live pipeline.
Video Stabilization
  • Attitude-aware stabilization fusing gimbal roll/pitch/yaw with IMU data, plus image-processing compensation for IMU/frame-rate jitter — clean video for both operators and matching algorithms.
Online Mosaicking & Live Georeferencing
  • Real-time frame-to-frame registration into a unified canvas, georeferenced from UAV pose and gimbal angles to update the map live during flight.
